Vala County Events information

We welcome you to participate in any upcoming events!

Visiting any of our must see attractions is a great way to experience the rich history and culture of Vala County. From the ancient ruins of the Old Vala Fortress to the vibrant art scene in downtown Vala, there is something for everyone.

Upcoming Events:

  • Vala County Fair - August 15-17, 2025: Join us for a weekend of fun, food, and festivities at the annual Vala County Fair.
  • Harvest Festival - September 20, 2025: Celebrate the bounty of the season with local produce, crafts, and entertainment.
  • Winter Wonderland Parade - December 5, 2025: Experience the magic of the holidays with our annual parade featuring floats, music, and more.
  • Festival of the Direwolf - August 15-17, 2025; Celebrate the majestic creatures that roam the lands
